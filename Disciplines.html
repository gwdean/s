1 Disciplines

The core of White's Theory of Disciplines can be found in the three diagrams on pages 355-357 of Identity and Control.

Each discipline is associated with a score along three parameters:

  • Involution (INV)
  • Dependence (DEP)
  • Differentiation (DIF)

Each of the discipline examples have a score on one of the parameters: High, Medium, or Low.

For example, within the INTERFACE discipline, the Battle-line units example score looks like this:

  • INV: High
  • DEP: High
  • DIF: Low

Whereas the City-neighborhoods example looks like this:

  • INV: High
  • DEP: High
  • DIF: High

Interestingly, the Functional department example also scores High on all three parameters, but is located within the ARENA discipline.
