1 Market Outcomes

This document is an analysis of the Market Outcomes listed on page 334-338 of Markets from Networks.

1.1 Meta Information

Five tables (A-1..5)

Each table corresponds to a different market.

(A-1 PARADOX) (A-2 ORDINARY) (A-3 CROWDED) (A-4 UNRAVELING) (A-5 TRUST)

Within each market, there are several submarkets:

(A-1 PARADOX 6) (A-2 ORDINARY 5) (A-3 CROWDED 4) (A-4 UNRAVELING 4) (A-5 TRUST 2)

There are a total of 21 submarkets.
